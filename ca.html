Question MissionUS Kahoot Habitica
Engagement: How engaging is the app? Is it visually appealing? Does it have sound and animation to capture user’s attention? Does it have good balance of challenge and reward? This website is very engaging and very visually appealing. It is colourful with many sounds animation which grabbed my attention right away. It offers many role-playing games that are interesting and educational. User is very engaged. The engagement is even enhanced by statistics and rewards user can receive during playing the games.(10/10) This app/website looks also very colourful and easy to use. The learning experience can be highly effective since user can also get competitive in the quizzes with other users. User can also create their own Kahoot quiz. The app feels more like a game then learning.(9/10) This app helps users achieve their goals and build healthy habits. The gamification in this keeps user motivated. User has their own pixel art character that they can update, dress, or change by using coins they get as a reward when they achieve the task they have set. Overall, the app looks nice and original(innovative).(8/10)
Progress tracking: How does the app track user’s progress? Does it provide feedback and recommendations to help users improve their performance? Does it use date to personalize the learning experience. This app provides some progress tracking. Your progress in the role play games is being saved. Every game also tracks your choices and answers trough out the story to show you the main character development. Other than that, I have not registered any progress tracking.(7/10) Kahoot provides good progress tracking in your learning. This app offers to personalize your account in terms of what you want to learn. It than practices these topics with you in modules trough quizzes and shows the progress in %.(9/10) This app comprehensive app tracking. User can receive awards and rewards, see completed tasks and see overall progress based on the level and health of his character.(10/10)
User experience: How user- friendly is the app? Is it easy to navigate and use? Does it have clear instructions and prompts? Does it have well- designed interface? This app is very easy to navigate, the interface is greatly designed, and I think majority of user would not have any issues with it during their first visits.(9/10) This app is easy to navigate, the interface design feels familiar so user thinks they can use right away without any specific instructions.(10/10) Habitica is easy to navigate after some time. I was slightly confused the first time I was using it and had to spend some time exploring the app.(6/10)
